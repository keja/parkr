The idea

Web-app for making it easier to pay for parking in Odense

How it works

• Scan QR code, or enter the zone and parking id
(located in front of car)

• Select duration, car and creditcard

• Press park

 

The technologies

Backend: Lumen based REST API (PHP)

Frontend: Require.js, TWIG, jQuery and LESS (JavaScript)
